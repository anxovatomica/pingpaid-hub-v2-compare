Lawyer-Specific Pain Points

⏱️ Time-Based Billing

6-minute increments, billable hours, non-billable time, and write-downs. Every minute needs to be accounted for.

📄 Flat Fee Structures

Fixed fees for specific matters: divorce, incorporation, trademark filing. You need to track profitability against the flat fee.

💰 Contingency Billing

Percentage of recovery, settlement amounts, and disbursement tracking. Complex math that changes with every case.

🏦 Trust Accounting

Client funds held in trust must be tracked separately from operating funds. Compliance is non-negotiable.

📋 UTBMS Codes

LEDES billing requires UTBMS task codes for every line item. Manual coding is tedious and error-prone.

📅 Retainer & Trust Replenishment

Clients pay retainers, you bill against them, and they need to replenish when low. Tracking this manually is a full-time job.
