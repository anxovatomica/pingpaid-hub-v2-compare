What Competitors Offer

FreshBooks: One customizable reminder when invoice is overdue. That's it. No late fees, no escalation, no legal letters.
QuickBooks: Two basic reminders. Slightly better than FreshBooks but still manual follow-up for everything after day 30.
Zoho: One basic reminder. Configurable but static. No late fee automation.
Wave: No automated reminders. Zero collection features. You handle everything manually.
PayPal: Payment reminders via PayPal, but no escalation, no late fees, no legal options.
